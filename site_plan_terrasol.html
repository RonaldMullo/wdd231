Site Plan - Your Space, Your Home, Your Lifestyle

Planning document for the Terrasol portfolio website (Terrasol 1 & Terrasol 2).

Site Name

Terrasol Communities

Reason for selection: The name groups a vision of modern communities in harmony with nature and a path from lot selection to home design and construction.

Optional domain: terrasol-communities.ec or terrasol-living.com

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a hub to present the Terrasol 1 and Terrasol 2 developments, describing amenities, lot availability (starting at 114 m²), integrated services (architectural design and construction), and calls‑to‑action such as “Schedule a Visit” or “Request Brochure.” It will include an inspiring homepage hero (“Your Canvas in Nature Awaits”), a projects portfolio, services, gallery, about, and contact.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

Scenario 1

“What are the key differences between Terrasol 1 and Terrasol 2 (e.g., pool, green areas, walking trails)?”

Scenario 2

“What is the minimum lot size and which basic services are included?”

Scenario 3

“Can I hire the home design and construction directly with you for a turnkey solution?”

Scenario 4

“How do I book a site tour or request a digital brochure?”
